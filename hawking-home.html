Stephen Hawking (1942-2018)

"However difficult life may seem, there is always something you can do and succeed at."

Born in Oxford in 1942, Stephen Hawking defied a devastating ALS diagnosis to become one of the most brilliant theoretical physicists of our time. His work on black holes, especially Hawking radiation, revolutionized our understanding of these cosmic phenomena. His bestselling book "A Brief History of Time" brought complex cosmological concepts to millions worldwide.
